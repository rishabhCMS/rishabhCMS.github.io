About
======
Hi! You have reached Rishabh. I am an empathetic and kind human, I originally hail from the mountain state called [Uttarakhand](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Uttarakhand,+India/@30.0655124,77.0680967,1003340m/data=!3m1!1e3!4m5!3m4!1s0x3909dcc202279c09:0x7c43b63689cc005!8m2!3d30.066753!4d79.0192997) in India. I spent most of my childhood in a tiny town called Dakpathar (I honestly love this place).  In 2010, I moved to Delhi to pursue a Bachelor's in Physics from the University of Delhi ([DU](http://du.ac.in/)) and subsequently moved to Chennai to get a M.S. degree in Physics from the Indian Institute of Technology-Madras ([IIT-M](https://www.iitm.ac.in/)). After graduating in 2016, I enrolled as a graduate student at the Catholic University of America ([CUA](https://www.catholic.edu/index.html)) in Washington D.C.

Currently, I am in my 5th year of a Ph.D. I am working on [searches for Dark Matter](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1mPXGVN2ObPNWaOcY7R6eN0LfQTYtYfn7CvT-5l9Yuys/edit?usp=sharing) at the [CMS](https://home.cern/science/experiments/cms) experiment at the Large Hardon Collider ([LHC](https://home.cern/science/accelerators/large-hadron-collider)) in Geneva, Switzerland.

How can I help you?
======
I am looking for opportunities to work in a data driven environment where I can put my scientific experience in the service of innovation, analysis, and business objectives.
